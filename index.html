Faryad

Ease For Everyone

‘The measure of a life, after all is not its duration, but its donation.’

Faryad is a platform designed for the needy that helps connect the ones in need with the donors via an application. The deserving ones or someone on behalf of them are free to post and request for something that they need for example ration, clothes, money etc. the request will be forwarded to organizations, welfare and people who are willing to donate. This is the most safest and convenient way of helping the ones in need.
